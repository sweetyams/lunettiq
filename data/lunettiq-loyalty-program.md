# Lunettiq Loyalty Program
**Source:** Loyalty Program Overview, October 2022

---

## Program Overview

Eyewear isn't just corrective — it's identity.

The Lunettiq Loyalty Program replaces transactional shopping with an ongoing relationship: monthly credits, yearly rotations, personalized perks, and cultural belonging.

**Main objectives:**
- Predictable recurring revenue (MRR)
- More frequent purchases (1 → 2 per year)
- Stronger lens and coating attach rate
- Distinct lifestyle positioning (status, belonging, culture)

---

## Tiers

### Essential — "The Coverage Tier"
**$19/mo or $199/yr**

For optical users who want coverage, convenience, and savings.

**Credits**
- Earns $15/month ($180/yr) in Lunettiq Credits
- Credits never expire while active and can be stacked
- Use on frames, lenses, coatings, or accessories

**Perks**
- One free tune-up or adjustment per year
- 20% trade-in value toward new frames through Second Sight
- $20 birthday credit
- Access to member pricing on accessories and lens upgrades

**Customer perception:** Affordable way to "belong." Feels like an insurance + membership hybrid.

**Financial logic:** If even 70% of credits are redeemed, you still net ~35% margin from unused value + recurring fees. This tier mainly exists to feed upgrades to CULT.

---

### CULT — "The Style Rotation Tier"
**$39/mo or $399/yr**

For people who view eyewear as part of their look — not a once-every-two-years purchase.

**Credits**
- Earns $30/month ($360/yr) in Lunettiq Credits
- Average frame cost after 1 year of saving: ~$120 out of pocket after credits

**Perks**
- Annual $40 Lens Refresh Credit for lens replacement or upgrades
- 30% trade-in value on old Lunettiq frames via Second Sight
- 25% off a new frame once per year when returning one
- Early access to new drops, collabs, and limited colours and one-offs
- Priority repair and service lane

**Customer perception:** Feels like they're building a rotation, not buying randomly. Their $39 monthly fee turns into "style credit."

**Financial logic:** At 70–80% redemption, net effective value delivered = $280–320 vs. $399 paid. Margin stays healthy because frames carry 60–65% margin, lens upgrades upsell, and trade-in resale runs through Second Sight. Sweet-spot tier — most members should land here.

---

### VAULT — "The Collector's Tier"
**$79/mo or $799/yr**

For fashion leaders, loyalists, and collectors who want access, not ownership.

**Credits**
- Earns $60/month ($720/yr) in Lunettiq Credits
- Enough to cover 1–1.5 full pairs per year with minimal top-up

**Perks**
- Annual $40 Lens Refresh Credit
- 35–40% trade-in value through Second Sight
- 1 Free Frame Rotation per year (equal or lesser MSRP) when returning a Lunettiq frame
- Member-only lens pricing (full margin preserved)
- Private member events, collab previews, styling sessions
- Ability to vote on new releases and colours

**Customer perception:** Feels like part of an exclusive circle — status, not discount.

**Financial logic:** $799 annual fee covers average COGS (~$200–250/frame + $80–100 lenses). Even after perks, margins remain 60%+ due to prepaid cash flow and resale loop. VAULT clients typically bring higher lifetime value through multi-pair purchases and influencer effect.

## Credit System and Redemption

### How Credits Work (All Tiers)

- Deposit on the 1st of each month
- Stackable, no expiry while active
- Redeem in-store or online
- Use for anything: frames, lenses, accessories, services, events
- Credits can stack with other perks (including the $40 Lens Refresh)
- 1 pause per 12 months allowed (up to 2 months)
- Cancel anytime; 60 days to use leftover credits

**Restrictions:** Non-transferable, no cash-out, cannot cover tax or shipping.

**Example:** A CULT member saves $30 × 6 = $180 in credits. Buys a $325 frame, pays $145. Lunettiq has already received $234 in membership fees — the system ensures upfront revenue.

## Lens Refresh, Second Sight, and Frame Rotation

### $40 Lens Refresh (CULT and VAULT)

- Once per 12-month cycle
- Use on: new lenses, coating upgrades, prescription changes
- Auto-resets yearly; stackable with credits
- Average margin after credit remains >55%
- Triggers a mid-cycle sale and keeps members coming back

---

### Second Sight Program

Circular trade-in system.

**Trade-in credit by tier:**
| Tier | Trade-in Value |
|---|---|
| Base (non-member) | 20% |
| CULT | 30% |
| VAULT | 35–40% |

**Operational flow:**
1. Staff grades frame (A/B/C)
2. A/B frames are refurbished — cleaned, repolished, repackaged
3. Listed under "Second Sight Archive" online
4. C-grade frames receive a recycling credit (5–10%) or are donated

**Why it works:**
- Re-engages past buyers
- Generates resale inventory with near-zero cost
- Strengthens the sustainability story

---

### Frame Rotation Program

**CULT**
- Once per year: trade one frame, get 25% off a new frame
- Stacks with credits and the Lens Refresh

**VAULT**
- Once per year: one free frame swap (equal or lesser MSRP)
- Lenses charged at member pricing

**Effect:** Turns eyewear into a collectible, upgradable item.
